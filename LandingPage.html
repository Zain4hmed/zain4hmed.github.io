History & Timeline

A brief timeline of milestones and initiatives that shaped our association.

1980 — Founding of the Association

A group of local advocates formed the Vijayawada Advocates Association to represent legal professionals and protect their interests within the district court.

1992 — Community Legal Aid Started

Initiated free legal aid clinics to help underserved citizens and expanded outreach with partner NGOs.

2001 — Mentorship & CLE Program

Introduced continuing legal education workshops and mentorship for junior advocates to improve professional standards.

2015 — Digitisation & Library Upgrade

Upgraded the association library with e-resources and ran workshops on digital filing and courtroom tech.

2023 — Expanded Outreach

Scaled legal literacy campaigning in schools and colleges and increased pro bono hours across the member base.
