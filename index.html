About Me

Well, hello there, folks! My name is Zain Ahmed, and I'm a software engineer with a penchant for perfection and optimized solutions. But don't worry, I'm not all code and no play - I also love making fun videos and dreaming up silly skits.

My love for problem-solving extends beyond the realm of coding - I also enjoy tackling mathematical puzzles and brain teasers. And when I'm not crunching numbers, you can find me tinkering with my autonomous RC car, which I've programmed to traverse graphs like a pro. No big deal, right?

But let's be real, life isn't all about work and engineering projects. When I was just a wee lad, I used to make videos with my cousins - and I still love it to this day. That's why I'm planning to start not one, but TWO YouTube channels. One will focus on technical skills and the mindset needed for success, while the other will be pure, unadulterated fun.

So if you're looking for someone who's equal parts nerd and goofball, you've found your guy. Let's solve some problems and have some fun, shall we?.
